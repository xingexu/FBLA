Frequently Asked Questions

Q1: What is Partners Repository?
Partners Repository is a platform where organizations can add and manage partnerships, enabling better collaboration and communication.
Q2: How do I add a new partner?
To add a new partner, go to the 'Add New Partner' page, fill in the required details, and submit the form.
Q3: Can I edit or delete a partner after adding them?
Yes, you can edit or delete a partner by navigating to the 'Partners' page and selecting the appropriate option for the partner you wish to modify.
Q4: What if I forget my password?
If you forget your password, click on the 'Forgot Password' link on the login page and follow the instructions to reset it.
Q5: How do I contact support?
For support, you can reach out to us via the 'Contact Us' page. We are here to help you with any issues or questions you may have.
